ABOUT LOOPMIDI
--------------

MidiWarp uses loopMIDI, a free virtual MIDI driver developed by Tobias Erichsen
(tobias-erichsen.de), to route MIDI signals between applications.

If loopMIDI is not already installed on your system, MidiWarp will install it
automatically and silently the first time it runs. No extra steps required.

By clicking Next and completing this installation, you acknowledge that loopMIDI
may be installed on your system as part of MidiWarp's setup process.

loopMIDI is free software. You can learn more at:
https://www.tobias-erichsen.de/software/loopmidi.html
